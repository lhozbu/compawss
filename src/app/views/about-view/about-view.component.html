About Compawss

Welcome to Compawss, your compass to find paws!

Our Mission

Every pet deserves a loving home. Our mission is to make pet adoption easier, more accessible, and fast. We've created a unique platform that acts as a meta search engine for pet adoption, connecting you with various adoption agencies and shelters across the globe.

How Compawss Works

Compawss utilizes technology to simplify the pet adoption search process. It aggregates information from multiple adoption APIs, creating a one-stop destination to search for furry companions. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a wise old cat, or any other lovable creature, Compawss is here to guide you on your adoption journey in an interactive and easy manner.

Why Choose Compawss?

  1. Diversity of Options: Compawss provides a wide array of options from different adoption agencies and shelters. This ensures that you can find the perfect pet that matches your lifestyle and preferences.
  2. Simplified Search: Our user-friendly interface allows you to easily search for pets based on criteria such as breed, size, and location (utilizing a map). Finding your new best friend has never been this straightforward.

Thank you for choosing Compawss as your companion in the journey of pet adoption. Together, let's make a difference in the lives of these wonderful creatures.
